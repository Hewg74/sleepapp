# Ruby — Custom Sleep Meditation

A meditation for Gene and his girlfriend, as Ruby faces her last stretch of life.

**Who Ruby is:**
- Norwich terrier, 5 years old, cancer
- Small, stocky, scruffy, fox-faced, red/tan
- Goofy, fiercely independent
- Chose her humans — walked onto their Maui property from a previous home that had not been kind to her
- Loves food (whole-body committed), walks to Poli Poli trail, the beach (vanishes and reappears), the forest (emerges with leaves stuck to her face)
- Recently visited Iao Valley for the first time — where her human prayed to Guan Yin to receive her when the time comes, and to guide her to a peaceful place

**Design principles:**
- Breath is the anchor threaded through every phase — never more than 20 seconds without returning to it
- Attention progressively moves inward: external → body → breath → heart → Ruby → love → release
- Specific memories delivered as sensory bath, not list — "sometimes an image comes" so different ones surface on different nights
- No active instructions. No "don't". The partner is ambient warmth.
- The spiritual work is honored as already done — the prayer has been placed, the valley holds it
- Grief and love are named as the same thing — neither needs to be solved tonight
- Designed for repeated listening — imagery is open enough for the listener's own Ruby to fill it in

## PHASE 1: ARRIVING (~3-4 min)
**Voice settings:** stability 0.45, speed 0.92, style 0.10
**Arc:** Meet them in activation → weight at contact points → Ruby as present in the house → partner's warmth → first breaths → permission

```
[calm] Let your body find a comfortable position. [long pause]

Whatever is here with you tonight... can be here. [pause] You can bring all of it to bed. The worry. The ache. The love that feels too big for your chest. [pause] Nothing about it needs to become smaller before you can rest. [long pause]

[night serene] Begin by noticing the weight of you. [pause] The places your body meets the mattress. [short pause] The back of your head on the pillow. [short pause] Your shoulders. [short pause] Your lower back. [short pause] The backs of your legs. [short pause] Your heels. [long pause]

Feel how much of you is being held right now. [pause] More than you realized. [long pause]

Somewhere in the house tonight... there is Ruby. [pause] Her small, stocky, completely-herself presence. [pause] Five kilos of conviction, curled up or stretched out, wherever she decided to be. [pause] She is part of what this home is made of. [long pause]

[night serene] And somewhere, not far from here, there is someone who loves her with you. [pause] The one who knows exactly what she means. [pause] You do not need to be in the same room for that love to be real. [pause] Two people, holding the same small warm life, across the air between two homes. [long pause]

You can let your eyes close... whenever they want to. [long pause]

[calm] Take one slow breath in through your nose. [pause] And let it go gently through your mouth. [long pause]

Notice the air at your nostrils. [pause] Cool on the way in. [pause] Warmer on the way out. [long pause]

One more. [short pause] In through the nose. [pause] Out through the mouth. [long pause]

And now... let your breath find its own rhythm. Nothing to control. Just let it do what it is already doing. [long pause]

[evening relaxed] Tonight, a love this big does not need to be politely put aside before you can sleep. [pause] It can lie here in the bed with you, as much a part of this moment as your breath. [long pause]

There is nothing you have to figure out tonight. [pause] Nothing you have to brace against. [pause] Nothing about the love, or the grief, or the fear needs to become different before you can rest. [long pause]

[sighs softly] You are allowed to just be here. [pause] Breathing. [pause] With Ruby somewhere close by, small and warm and entirely hers. [long pause]

Take one more slow breath in. [pause] And let it all go. [long pause]

Good. [short pause] You have arrived.
```
